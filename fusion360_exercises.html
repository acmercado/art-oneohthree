phase 1, part 2: "do-it-yourself"

title:

Object #1 - Anything

date:

10/08/2020

medium:

3D Model (Fusion 360 - Desktop App)

description/process:

I didn't know what to make, so I made a layout of my room and stylized it the way I wish I could renovate my room to look like. I used solids, sketches, extrusions, press/pulls, combine, fillet, draft, holes, and changed its appearance. Unfortunately I had trouble with the blanket after I combined bodies but when rendered, it didn't look too bad.

title:

Object #2 - Number Sequence

date:

10/08/2020

medium:

3D Model (Fusion 360 - Desktop App)

description/process:

(I wasn't quite sure if I was doing this correctly). I first created a "gem-like" shape (the small white one on the side), copied it multiple times, then rescaled it in different ways. I stacked up the pieces on top of each other in different areas to make the entire object look more like a gem cluster. To give it more of a "gem" look, I fillet some sides to make some look asymmetrical. I took a while changing the appearance to mulitple colors.
