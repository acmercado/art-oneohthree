"microbox"

title:

microbox

date:

10/05/2020

medium:

breadboard, adafruit metro mini board, speaker, 2 leds, toggle switch, momentary button, tuning potentiometer, and sliding potentiometer (1 220 ohm & 3 10k ohm resistors).

description/process:

(in the third photo above, circled in green is where the toggle switch ended up going. it wasn't pictured with the breadboard outside of the box because it wasn't working until i resoldered the wires to it).

. . .

for my microbox, it plays "bippity boppity boo" from cinderella. the toggle switch turns on the box. the led on the right blinks along to the tempo of the song. the tuning potentiometer (pot for short) changes its speed. (only noticeable when the tuning pot is at its fast tempo), the led on the left can be dimmed or brightened using the sliding pot. while holding the momentary button, the box plays a different melody of the song and the tuning pot can change its speed as well.
