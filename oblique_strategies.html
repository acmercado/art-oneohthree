"oblique strategies"

title:

Dinner

date:

08/25/2020

medium:

png file

description:

Our group's oblique strategies were (1) face with a choice, do both; (2) emphasize differences; and (3) a very small, object, its center. We decided to go with our first oblique strategy -- face with a choice, do both -- as the main idea for all of our projects. For a sense of "cohesion" to our project as well, we decided to base our art on food with each of us being assigned to specific a meal time such as breakfast, lunch, dinner and dessert.

. . .

I was assigned to the meal-time, Dinner. My "face with a choice, do both" is the choice of having steak and mashed potatoes or shrimp. I would have added more shrimp but I had program difficulties when creating my art piece.
