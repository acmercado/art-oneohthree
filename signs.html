phase 1: "sign series"

title:

Every Day Signs

date:

10/27/2020

medium:

JPG Imgs, Adobe's PhotoShop and Illustrator

map of signs

the red dots correspond to the order of the signs shown from right to left.

description/process:

for this phase, I wanted to find places that a "covid-19" related sign would be placed but instead I ended up going to areas that not only relate to myself, but possibly other people. I used Illustrator to make my signs and PhotoShop to make it seem as if they were actually in the place the pictures were taken.

. . .

the first sign is at the "Parma Sister City Park" and it's one of the many parks in my neighborhood that had their basketball hoops/backboards removed in order to prevent the spread of COVID-19.

. . .

the second place already has an existing sign but I changed it to be more relevant to the drivers of today.

. . .

the third destination is technically a "road ends here" area, but many people (teens and young adults) come here to cruise around on their skateboards, longboards, bikes, and roller skates for fun.

. . .

the fourth area has a sign that showcases what could happen if one didn't slow down before encountering the "speed bump".
